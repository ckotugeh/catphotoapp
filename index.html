Recipe

This a pan cake recipe

Ingredients

  • sugar
  • salt
  • 3 eggs
  • clear water
  • cooking oil
  • 1kg of ngano floor

Instructions

  1. put 5ml warm water into bowl
  2. prepare eggs in a dish
  3. add to the water
  4. add 1//2 tea spoon of salt and sugar stir the mixture well
  5. add 4 cups of ngano floor
  6. palce pan to heat
  7. rub cooking oil on pan
  8. add a well mixed semiliquid floor mixture
